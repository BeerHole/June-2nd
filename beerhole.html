HOW TO PLAY

⚠

Disclaimer

  • House Rules: Rules may vary depending on who is playing. Generally, the local "house rules" established by the host or venue prior to starting the game are what should be followed.
1

Equipment & Setup

  • Boards: Place two Beerhole Boards across from one another.
  • Holes: Utilize the 10 permanent holes built into each board.
  • Bags: Provide each team with two small bean bags for tossing.
  • Discs: Keep 10 scoring discs per board nearby to cover the holes.
  • Drinks: Players keep their own personal beverages on the side.
  • Teams: Play with two teams, usually two players each.
2

Gameplay Rules

  • First Turn: Determine who tosses first via Rock-Paper-Scissors or "Eye-to-Eye" (tossing while looking directly into an opponent's eyes).
  • Turn Structure: Each team member tosses one bag per turn — two total tosses per team.
  • Foul Line: Players must keep feet behind the front edge of their own board when tossing.
  • Covering Holes: When a bag lands in a hole, the defending team places a disc over it and takes a drink.
  • Bag Returns: If both players make their tosses in the same turn, they get the bags back and receive two bonus tosses.
3

Specialized Mechanics

  • Re-racking: Since holes are permanently fixed, teams can strategically leave certain holes uncovered during scoring adjustments to simulate standard re-rack formations.
  • Teammate Assists: If a bag sits on the rim of an uncovered hole, the second teammate can knock it in with their own toss.
  • Redemption: Once a team covers all 10 holes, the losing team gets one final turn to toss until they miss. Cover all remaining holes and you stay alive — game continues until one team misses a rebuttal.
