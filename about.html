Our Story

BeerHole didn't start in a boardroom. It started in a garage, the same place most great ideas do.

How It Started

Built From The Ground Up

BeerHole was invented by us — a group of friends who wanted something better to play at backyard hangouts, tailgates, and pregames. We looked around and thought: beer pong is great, cornhole is great, but what if you combined the best parts of both into something totally new?

What started as an idea in our garage turned into a game that everyone who played immediately wanted to own. The rules came naturally. The competition was instant. The fun was undeniable. We knew we had something.

We built the first sets by hand, tested them with friends, refined the rules, and kept pushing until BeerHole was exactly what we knew it could be — the game that owns every backyard, tailgate, and party it shows up at.

Who Makes It

Piggy Pit Woodworking

Every BeerHole set is manufactured by Piggy Pit Woodworking — a craftsman operation that shares our commitment to quality. Each board is built to last, built to perform, and built to look great in any setting.

When we set out to bring BeerHole to life, we didn't want something mass-produced and forgettable. Partnering with Piggy Pit means every set that leaves the shop is something we're proud to put our name on.
